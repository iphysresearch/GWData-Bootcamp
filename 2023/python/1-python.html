General Facts
Shanghai is a city in East China; it is the largest city of the Peoples Republic of China and the 8th largest city in the world. Due to its rapid growth of the last two decades, it has again become a global city; it is also known as the Paris of the East. Shanghainese kids start learning English in the first grade, like it or not, English is now a compulsory course for all pupils in Shanghai. In a decades time, everyone in the city may speak English or a hybrid language of Chinese and English, known as Chinglish.
